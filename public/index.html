OSCAR is a tool to create custom and beautiful Graphical User Interfaces to control interactive installations

For tutorials, feedback, and help you can refer to our webpage and open an issue. For problems with the builder itself, open an issue on the main OSCAR repository or drop an email to hellooscarosc@gmail.com

Being a free and open source project contributors and supporters are extremely welcome. OSCAR is looking for collaborators all the time. Share your project and let us be part of them, please let us know! hellooscarosc@gmail.com
